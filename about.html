Our Story

From EnvisionRecruit
to Kavalan Digital.

We started in Taipei over 15 years ago with a simple belief: international education institutions deserved a marketing partner who truly understood the local markets they were trying to reach — not a global agency applying a one-size-fits-all playbook.

Under the name EnvisionRecruit, we built our reputation connecting schools from the U.S., Canada, U.K., Europe, and Asia with students, agents, and counselors across Taiwan and Southeast Asia. We grew a proprietary database of over 5,880 contacts. We ran hundreds of campaigns. We organized fairs, workshops, and coffee chats that filled rooms.

In 2025, we rebranded as Kavalan Digital — a name that reflects where we come from and what we stand for. Kavalan is the indigenous name for the Yilan region of Taiwan, the place where our roots run deep. It speaks to local knowledge, cultural fluency, and a forward direction.

The rebrand is more than a new name. It's a commitment to bringing even sharper strategic thinking, broader regional reach, and more sophisticated digital capabilities to every client we serve.

Why "Kavalan"?

Kavalan (噶瑪蘭) is the name of the indigenous people of the Yilan plain in northeastern Taiwan — one of the most fertile and beautiful regions of the island.

The name carries the spirit of deep local roots combined with an outward-looking perspective. The Kavalan people were known as skilled navigators and traders — a fitting metaphor for what we do: navigate complex markets and connect institutions to the students they're looking for.
