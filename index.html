About Kavalan Digital

Formerly EnvisionRecruit.
Evolved for today.

We are a digital marketing agency with over 15 years of experience serving education programs from the U.S., Canada, U.K., Europe, and Asia. Our clients are institutions — universities, business schools, and education brands — who trust us to grow their enrollment in markets we know intimately.

We are headquartered in Taipei, with on-the-ground presence across Vietnam, Cambodia, Myanmar, and Thailand. We work on a marketing-fee model: your success is our mandate, not a commission afterthought.

Why Kavalan?

Kavalan is the indigenous name for the Yilan region of Taiwan — the place where we're rooted, and the spirit of local knowledge that we bring to every campaign.

We don't just translate your marketing. We localize your strategy: understanding the platforms, the gatekeepers, and the cultural nuances that move prospective students and their families toward a decision.
